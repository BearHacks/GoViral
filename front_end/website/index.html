We work on software projects with AI/ML to help people around the world.

Our mission is help youtubers, big and small, to make the most money by using our enriching algorithm to preview the projected profits of their video and allowing them to change key aspects of their video such as title and thumbnails in order to be as profitable as possible and grow their channel.
