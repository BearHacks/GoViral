Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does your product cost?

Right now, out product is in BETA, so it is completely free to use. However, we cannot guarantee 100% accurate results until the product is fully developed. Once it is, prices can be found above, starting at $3 per month.

When will your product be officially released?

We do not have an exact time yet, as there are still many bugs to work out. But as soon as we have a release date, we'll be sure to let everyone know!

Where can I use the product?

Press the "API" Button at the top of your screen to be directed to the product.

Who is Go Viral made for?

While Go Viral specifically focuses on youtubers, the customization proccess can be applied to other platform users as well. In the future, we plan to incorporate multiple platforms such as Twitch and Mixer as well.
